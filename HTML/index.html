A Legacy of Compassion

Ratan Tata — India's most beloved industrialist — was not just a business icon. He was a lifelong champion of stray dogs and animal welfare. He set up shelter facilities inside Tata headquarters, personally funded animal hospitals, and never missed an opportunity to advocate for street animals. His final social media posts were often about rescuing dogs.

"I may have lived a life less ordinary, but the dogs who came into my life made it extraordinary."
— Ratan Tata
